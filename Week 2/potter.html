Harry Potter Characters

This is a description of the leading Harry Potter characters

Harry Potter

Main character of the series, son of James Potter and Lily Evans, Gryffindor student, Gryffindor Quidditch Seeker and captain, leader of Dumbledore's Army.

Hermione Granger

Muggle-born Gryffindor, student in Harry's year, one of Harry's best frineds, founder of Dumbledore's Army, Gryffindor Prefect.

Ron Weasley

Harry's close friend, youngest son of Arthur and Molly Weasley, Gryffindor Quidditch Keeper, school prefect, a member of Dumbledore's Army.
